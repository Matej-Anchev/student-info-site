Семинари & Настани

Институтот редовно организира семинари, конференции и академски настани кои ги собираат математичари, истражувачи и студенти. Овие собирања го поттикнуваат интелектуалниот размен и ги прикажуваат најновите развој во математиката и нејзините применувања.

Ninth Seminar: Mathematics and Applications

The annual "Mathematics and Applications" seminar series celebrates mathematical achievements and explores contemporary applications of mathematics in science, technology, and industry.

Event Details:

  • Date: 13 March 2026
  • Location: Institute of Mathematics, PMF Building
  • Format: 20-minute presentations from emerging and established researchers
  • Topics: Theoretical and applied mathematics, computational methods

Important Deadlines:

  • 4 March 2026 – Deadline for abstract submissions
  • 9 March 2026 – Registration deadline (extended)
  • 11 March 2026 – Final registration cutoff

Mathematical Omnibus Series

Participants in the "Mathematics and Applications" seminars are invited to submit papers for publication in the Mathematical Omnibus series—a collection of peer-reviewed scientific and popular mathematical articles.

Submission Requirements:

  • Original research or review article
  • Adherence to formatting guidelines
  • Macedonian fonts (SKOLA font family) for Macedonian submissions
  • Following the provided paper template
